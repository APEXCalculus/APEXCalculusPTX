ADJUSTMENTS FOR YOUTUBE QR CODES IN MARGIN

1. Replace \tcbset{ qrstyle/.style={bwminimalstyle, hbox} }
with \tcbset{ qrstyle/.style={bwminimalstyle, left=25pt} }

2. Replace "YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=*"
with "https://youtu.be/*"

3. Replace \mono{https://youtube.com/watch\_videos?video_ids=*}
with  \mono{https://youtube.com/watch\_videos?}  \mono{video\_ids=*}
